The flow

From intent to brief
in under two minutes.

No tabs, no comparison spreadsheets. One description, one brief — with everything ranked and ready to act on.

01

Describe your trip

Enter your destination, travel dates, group size, and budget. Add anything that matters — pace, occasion, dietary preferences, what's already covered.

02

Get a tailored brief

Dossier researches across all four verticals — flights, stays, dining, and experiences — and returns ranked recommendations personalized to how you travel.

03

Book directly

Tap any recommendation to open the provider's booking page, pre-filled with your details. You compare live rates and complete the booking on their platform.

Why Dossier

Built for the decision,
not the research.

✦

Decision-ready, not a data dump

Every recommendation includes the context to act on it — pricing, rationale, fit to your trip. Nothing in the brief exists to fill space.

◈

Personalized over time

Dossier remembers how you travel. Each brief gets sharper — accommodation style, pace, dietary habits, home airports.

◎

All four verticals, one brief

Flights, hotels, restaurants, and activities researched in parallel and returned as a single cohesive brief — with a budget summary.

→

You stay in control

About

Dossier is a consumer iOS app built and maintained by Obinna Oparah. It is currently in early access, distributed to a small tester cohort ahead of an App Store release.

The goal: minimize the gap between "we want to travel" and "trip is booked" — without offloading the final decision to an algorithm.
